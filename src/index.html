Play & Earn

Characters are the
new Land

Why should a metaverse be based around land if the real world is centered around humans like us? In the character-centered metaverse Chibbies, players can create unique characters through breeding and then use their chibbies to interact with other players, play games and earn tokens.

What can I do
with Chibbies?

We settled on a 3D characters, not just because they look much cooler than the typical 2D NFT. Building on a 3D Character Framework gives us maximum flexibility for expanding the Chibbies ecosystem. Breeding is only the first step by creating your characters.

You will be able to level your Chibbies through playing a range of games. Each game will feature unique items, that can only be obtained within that game but then be used in all games. Customize your chibbie by equipping all kinds of items and accessories.

All games are bridged together by the characters themselves and a single utility token that can be earned through playing the games

Attack Legendary Defense Common Special Epic

3D Characters = 3Dimensional rarity system

When designing the Platform we gave every Chibbie not one but three rarities. With every game entering the Chibbies Metaverse, these rarities are mapped to a single attribute of that game.

Blockchain

Why blockchain?

The use of NFT (Non fungible Token) ensures the true ownership of your Game Items, meaning that all game items also exist independently of the game and belong to you. They are not owned by us, like in typical game franchises

SmartContracts, handle all in-game transactions, such as the breeding. These are calculated on the blockchain and ensure a secure and correct outcome each and every time

Wtf is WAX Blockchain?

You’ve used blockchain before, but absolutely hate gas fees? - Meet the WAX Blockchain

WAX Blockchain was already optimized towards fast and low fee trading for digital goods, even before the term NFT existed

Originally built on Ethereum, WAX’s architecture was changed 5 years ago to avoid high gas fees and long transaction times

WAX is already used by over 10million other accounts franchises such as:
Funko, Streetfighter, Spiderman, Topps, Deadmau5

Learn how to create a WAX account and securely collect NFTs all from your browser, no additional Software or Extensions required!
