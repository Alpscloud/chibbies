What can I do
with Chibbies?

01

Breed & Collect:

Every Chibbie features a set of Physical and Performance Traits. Through strategic breeding, you will be able to improve your bloodline and create crazy combinations.

02

Games & Items:

Breeding is only the first step. Chibbies is designed as a Gaming Platform with the Characters as a central part. A range of Games will be launched on top of Chibbies and give your Characters utility.
Each game will feature unique items, that can only be obtained within that game but then be used in most games. Customize your Chibbie by equipping all kinds of items and accessories.

03

Earn

All games are bridged together by a single utility token that can be earned through playing the games. Select your favorite game(s), optimize your strategy, breed targeted characters - and earn!

Blockchain

Why Blockchain?

The use of NFT (Non fungible Token) ensures the true ownership of your Game Items, meaning that all game items also exist independently of the game and belong to you. They are not owned by us, like in typical game franchises

SmartContracts, handle all in-game transactions, such as the breeding. These are calculated on the blockchain and ensure a secure and correct outcome each and every time

Wax Blockchain

You’ve used blockchain before, but you can’t stand gas fees? - Meet the WAX Blockchain

WAX Blockchain was already optimized towards fast and low fee trading for digital goods, even before the term NFT existed. Originally built on Ethereum, WAX’s architecture was changed 5 years ago to avoid high gas fees and long transaction times

WAX is already used by over 10million other accounts and franchises such as:
Funko, Streetfighter, Spiderman, Topps, Deadmau5
